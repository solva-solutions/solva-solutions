## About THORChain

THORChain is a decentralized cross-chain liquidity protocol that enables native asset swaps between major blockchains — including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other UTXO and EVM chains — without wrapped tokens or bridges. Liquidity providers deposit native assets into THORChain's continuous liquidity pools and earn swap fees, while validators (nodes) bond RUNE as collateral to secure the network and process cross-chain transactions. RUNE is the native token that acts as the settlement asset in every swap, and is used for bonding, liquidity provision, and governance.
